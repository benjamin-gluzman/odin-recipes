Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made of a round, flat crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. It’s baked in an oven until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Originating from Naples, pizza has evolved into countless regional and international variations. Today, it’s enjoyed worldwide as both a quick meal and a comfort food shared with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil, then knead until smooth. Let it rise until it doubles in size.
  2. Preheat the oven: Set it to around 475–500°F (245–260°C) so it’s hot enough for a crispy crust.
  3. Shape the dough: Roll or stretch it into a circle on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Add toppings: Spread tomato sauce evenly, sprinkle cheese, and add your desired toppings.
  5. Bake: Cook for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Cool and serve: Let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy!
