Cake

Description

Cake is a sweet, baked dessert made from a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, and butter or oil. It’s often light and fluffy, with a soft texture that can be flavored with vanilla, chocolate, fruit, or spices.

Cakes are usually layered or frosted and served for celebrations like birthdays and weddings. They come in many varieties, from simple sponge cakes to rich, creamy cheesecakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Frosting

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients – Gather flour, sugar, eggs, butter (or oil), baking powder, milk, and any flavoring (like vanilla or cocoa).
  2. Preheat oven – Set your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease or line your cake pan.
  3. Mix wet ingredients – Beat butter and sugar until fluffy, then add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Add dry ingredients – In another bowl, mix flour and baking powder, then gradually add to the wet mixture with milk.
  5. Bake the cake – Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 25–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool and frost – Let the cake cool before adding frosting or decorations.
